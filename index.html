About Me

UX/CX Designer & Graphic Designer

I'm a versatile professional skilled in UX design, graphic design, and CX design(marketing). With a wide-ranging skill set, I seamlessly transition between these areas to create impactful user experiences.

As a UX designer, I focus on understanding users' needs. Through research and user personas, I ensure design decisions resonate. Wireframing, prototyping, and testing refine interactions for intuitive products. As a graphic designer, I use creativity to craft stunning solutions. From brand logos to captivating marketing materials, my designs engage. I also offer comprehensive marketing services, blending insights into user behavior and design principles. This mix drives eye-catching campaigns that deliver results.

This adaptability, coupled with my commitment to excellence, ensures that whether I'm designing a user interface, creating compelling visuals, or driving marketing initiatives, I consistently deliver experiences that captivate, resonate, and leave a lasting impact.
