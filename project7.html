Brazil World Cups Infographic

Using an infographic to show Brazil's World Cup success visually presents key information about the year's Brazil's achievements in the FIFA World Cup tournaments. The infographic was designed to represent a game in Brazil called "button table" wherein the background is the soccer field and the buttons represent the players shown each year of the word cup's victory.
