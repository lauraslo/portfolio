Power Up

The Corporate Identity Book I meticulously crafted captures the essence of my visionary brand. It all began with a comprehensive exploration of my company's identity, answering fundamental questions that laid the foundation for its unique character. With a clear mission in mind, I conceived an imaginative and distinctive company name that seamlessly encapsulated my aspirations. I unleashed my ideas onto paper, sketching out an array of 20 thumbnail concepts, each embodying a potential facet of my brand's identity. With a refined selection in hand, I embarked on bringing my top 3-5 sketches to life, digitally breathing them into existence using Adobe Illustrator.

To ensure adaptability and clarity, I developed a black and white version of the logo and horizontal and vertical variations, catering to diverse display spaces and mediums. The infusion of color I selected HEX, RGB, and CMYK codes to establish a consistent color palette across digital and print platforms, aligning with my brand's persona and message. And using different layout(business card, instagram ads, skyscraper, superimpose and thumbnails) to help the visual representation of the brand's identity, values, and message. It shapes how the brand is perceived, helps it connect with the target audience, and ultimately contributes to its overall growth and success.
