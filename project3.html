Bakery App

In this project, I created a bakery eCommerce app. To showcase the products, I handpicked twenty bakery treats and photo shoot, ensuring consistency in editing to present them on the app. Look for others bakeries apps and website for inspirations to help creating the wireframes for the app, attempting to optimize efficient navigation and elevate the user experience.

Empathy Map & Personas

Understanding the significance of defining my target users, I dig into exploring various potential users. As a key aspect of this exploration, I crafted a empathy map and three user personas.

Challenges:

  • Selecting twenty bakery products.
  • Editing all the product to look consistent.
  • Using the assignment provided logo, color palette and typography.
  • Time management to finish all the parts on the time, conciliating with the others classes.
